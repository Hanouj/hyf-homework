Ingredients
- Dried chickpeas
- Fresh herbs: fresh parsley, cilantro, and dill
- Onion: I typically use yellow onions, but white or red onions would work.
- Garlic: for best flavor, use fresh garlic cloves.
- Kosher salt and pepper: to taste.
- Spices: cumin, coriander, and a little cayenne pepper. Along with the fresh herbs.
- Baking powder
